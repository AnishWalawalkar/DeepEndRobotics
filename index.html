Coral Reefs

Coral Reefs are home to hundreds of thousands of oceam organisms and provide benefits such as coastal protection and erosion prevention. The estimated economic value of the world's coral reef ecosystems total over $30 billion annually. However, these fragile ecosystems are under constant threat from climate change and increased ocean acidity

Our Mission

The first step in conservation is data collection. Current surveying methodologies involve the use of commercial dive teams, and high-level satellite or aerial photography. Our mission is to automate the current methodology of coral reef surveying and improve efficiency and data accuracy.

Our Solution

We are designing an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle for more cost-effective coral reef surveying.

Our Strategy
